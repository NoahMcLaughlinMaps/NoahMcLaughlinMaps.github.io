USA Air and Drone Strikes

The Map: Displays all reported American air and drone strikes on Afghanistan, documented by the Bureau of Investigative Journalism, from the start of 2015 up until November 12, 2016. All points are referenced based on the assosiated political district. During this time frame there have been approximately 848 total reported strikes, 2680 reported people killed, and 152 reported civilian casualties.

Data Source: The Bureau of Investigative Journalism, Covert Drone War. 2016.
